Our Story

Woven With Legacy

A.S. Babu Sah (Babu Sarees Private Limited) was started as a retail business in 1974, and the exclusive selections and traditional designs stand apart — indicating the experience and passion of four generations of a single family dedicated to the art of Kanchipuram silk.

We have our own handloom factory with in-house weavers and a state-of-the-art dyeing facility capable of producing over one lakh colour combinations. Every saree that leaves our showroom carries the soul of authentic Kanchipuram craftsmanship.
