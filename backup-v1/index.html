Our Story

Woven With Legacy

A.S. Babu Sah (Babu Sarees Private Limited) was started as a retail business in 1974, and the exclusive selections and traditional designs stand apart — indicating the experience and passion of four generations of a single family dedicated to the art of Kanchipuram silk.

We have our own handloom factory with in-house weavers and a state-of-the-art dyeing facility capable of producing over one lakh colour combinations. Every saree that leaves our showroom carries the soul of authentic Kanchipuram craftsmanship.

Our Promise

Why A.S. Babu Sah

Over fifty years of unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity, and the timeless art of Kanchipuram silk weaving.

Own Handloom Factory

We manufacture our sarees from raw silk to finished product — ensuring unmatched quality control at every step of the weaving process.

1 Lakh+ Colour Combinations

Our in-house dyeing works produce over one lakh unique colour combinations, bringing your dream saree to life in any shade imaginable.

New Arrivals Every Day

Fresh collections arrive at our showroom daily. Every visit reveals something new — exclusive patterns born from the loom of tradition.

Pure Zari & Jari

We use authentic zari and pure jari in our sarees, maintaining the heritage standard of Kanchipuram silk that has been celebrated for centuries.

Online Shopping via WhatsApp

Browse our full collection via live WhatsApp Video Call from anywhere in India. We also offer door-step shipping across the country.

Four Generations of Expertise

Since 1974, our family has continuously refined the art of saree selection. Every recommendation carries decades of curated knowledge.
